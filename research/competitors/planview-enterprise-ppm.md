# Planview & Enterprise PPM Class

## Planview
Enterprise **portfolio and work management** — strategy to delivery.

### Typical modules / themes (SECONDARY + product pages)
- **Project Portfolio Management** — prioritize by strategic alignment, portfolio performance  
- **AdaptiveWork** — project execution, Gantt/WBS, templates, resource workloads, workflow automation, customer account profiles  
- Resource management across portfolio  
- Strategic planning / OKR-style alignment  
- Work management for teams  

### GUI
- Executive portfolio tiles, demand boards, roadmap views  
- Heavier admin configuration than startup PM tools  
- Role: PMO / EPMO / IT portfolio — not jobsite foreman  

### Why it matters vs Tornix
Tornix **Strategy + PMO Dashboard + BSC** aims at this layer. Planview is the global enterprise proof that **strategy↔project linkage** is a paid product category. Tornix differentiates by construction DNA + Arabic + AI agents; Planview differentiates by enterprise process maturity and scale.
