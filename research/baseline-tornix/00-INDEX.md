## What is Tornix?

Tornix is an **AI-powered construction / enterprise PMO (Project Management Office) platform**.  
Arabic name: **تورنكس**.  
Tagline (EN): *Your Workflow Is Slowing You Down. Tornix Fixes That.*  
Tagline (AR): *سير عملك يبطئك — تورنكس يحل ذلك.*  
Promise: *Ten years of productivity for your upcoming projects* / *عشر سنوات من الإنتاجية لمشاريعك القادمة.*

It targets **MENA contractors and project organizations**, with full **Arabic RTL + English**, mobile apps, and AI agents woven into every module (not bolted on).
