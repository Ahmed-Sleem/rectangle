## Problem statement (from Ailigent / Tornix materials)

Big programs don’t fail loudly — they slip quietly. Signals get buried in spreadsheets, chat threads, and status decks. By the time a risk shows up in a monthly report, cost is already locked in.

### Pain points called out

1. **Fragmented tools** — Tasks, budgets, approvals, and documents live in separate systems that never agree.
2. **Manual reporting** — Teams spend days assembling status reports that are outdated when sent.
3. **Blind spots** — Risks, delays, and cost overruns noticed too late to react.

## Solution

One AI-native workspace for the full PMO stack:

- Live executive command center  
- Projects, tasks, Gantt/CPM, EVM  
- Risks & issues with AI scoring  
- Costs, procurement, documents, approvals  
- Strategy / BSC / KPIs  
- Collaboration (chat, mail, meetings, calendar)  
- Autonomous **AI Twin / Super Agent** and domain agents  

### How it works (Ailigent 4 steps)

1. **Connect** — Bring projects, teams, costs, and documents into one structured workspace.  
2. **Configure** — Model org structure, policies, approval flows, and KPIs with no-code builders.  
3. **Run** — Teams work day-to-day while dashboards and reports stay live automatically.  
4. **Escalate** — AI Twin monitors, predicts risk, and surfaces only exceptions that need a decision.

## Target audience

- MENA / Middle East contractors and construction firms  
- Enterprise project managers and PMO offices  
- Executives needing portfolio visibility  
- Site engineers, investors, multi-stakeholder programs  
- Organizations that need **Arabic RTL** as a first-class experience  
- Teams replacing or complementing Primavera P6, MS Project, Procore-style stacks  

## Positioning pillars (“Why choose us”)

1. **AI-powered** — Not a tool; a teammate that outthinks every problem. (Super Agent, take actions, review progress, create reports, memory approach, perfect performance, report issues, notify users.)
2. **AI strategy** — From boardroom objectives to site actions. (Goals → KPI tree → link projects → benefits tracking → team alignment → predict outcomes → detect deviations.)
3. **AI Gantt** — Every task, dependency, delay seen before it hits. (Auto schedule, critical path, progress, baseline compare, P6 import, delay detection, resource leveling, AI forecasts.)
4. **AI risk intelligence** — Catches risks the team missed weeks earlier. (Early detection, Monte Carlo, risk register, critical-path impact, probability score, alert teams, suggest actions, track resolution.)
5. **Agents that don’t sleep** — Schedule, alert, report, and decide overnight. (Scheduling, cost, risk, reporting, documents, memory, PMO agents.)
