### App Store description (summary of feature bullets)

Tornix — AI-powered project management for enterprise teams. Smart automation, instant insights, collaboration for executives and PMs.

- **Executive Dashboard** — progress, budget, risks, task completion, team sentiment  
- **Projects & Tasks** — milestones, schedules, dependencies, comments, files, reminders  
- **Risk & Issue Management** — severity, likelihood, mitigations; AI scoring & priorities  
- **Cost Control** — baseline vs actual, change orders, invoices, cash flow; CPI, SPI, EVM live  
- **Team Sentiment Analysis** — automatic sentiment + recommendations  
- **AI Assistant** — Q&A, risk analysis, task suggestions, management plans, meeting summaries  
- **Meetings with Auto-Transcription** — transcripts + smart summaries  
- **Approvals** — expenses, documents, change orders; real-time approver notifications  
- **Team & Permissions** — roles, workload, fine-grained project/org permissions  
- **Documents & Contracts** — files, contracts, payment schedules, blueprint auto-extract  
- **Time Tracking** — hours per task, exportable reports  
- **Real-time Notifications** — push; optional Telegram  
- **Full Arabic Support (RTL)**  
- **Integrations** — Google & Apple Sign-In, enterprise SSO, open APIs
