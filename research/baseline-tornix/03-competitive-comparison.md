## Narrative takeaway (for reuse)

Tornix positions itself as:

1. The only option in the set with a **full AI stack** (BOQ scheduling, agents, strategy, automated reporting).  
2. Stronger **modern PMO analytics** (EVM + executive views) than the classic tools as they score them.  
3. The clear winner on **Arabic RTL and MENA contractor UX**.  
4. A potential **replacement or complement** to P6/MS Project/Procore rather than a pure scheduling clone.

## Other competitive context (external, not from tornix.ai table)

- Classic enterprise scheduling: Primavera P6, MS Project  
- Construction collaboration / field: Procore (and peers like Autodesk Construction Cloud, PlanGrid historically, etc.)  
- Regional angle: few deep Arabic-first AI PMO products — central to their “صفر منافسين بالعربية” claim
